Krishna is a major deity in Hinduism, revered as the eighth avatar (incarnation) of the god Vishnu, and also worshiped as the Supreme God himself.He is the god of protection, compassion, tenderness, and love, and is widely celebrated across India and beyond. Krishna is a central figure in the Mahabharata and the Bhagavad Gita, where he serves as a warrior, teacher, and philosopher.
